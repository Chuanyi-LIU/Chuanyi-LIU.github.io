Damage Evaluation of Underground Projects Based on Artificial Intelligence

¬∑ Supported by State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Manufacturing of Advanced Construction Machinery.
¬∑ Integrated reinforcement learning algorithms with finite element analysis simulation software ABAQUS, developed a Prioritized Experience Replay + Deep Q-Network (PER+DQN) algorithm, applied them to design a Cell-based Collision Energy-absorption Structure (CCES).
¬∑ Established key collision energy absorption metrics of CCES and used them to design the reward function, encouraging the reinforcement learning agent to use ABAQUS to optimize CCES.
¬∑ Manufactured CCES through 3D printing, conducted actual collision experiments through self-developed MSC, verifying the significant advantages of CCES in energy absorption efficiency and reducing peak force.
